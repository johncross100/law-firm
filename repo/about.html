About Us

Brief History

The firm of Boma Obuoforibo & Co. was established in 1977 in Port Harcourt. Over the years we have acquired a highly diversified client base and we offer services to multinationals in diverse sectors of the economy, Oil and Gas Companies, Commercial Banking, Foreign Institutions/Companies, Property developers, etc.

We have a flexible, responsive approach bringing years of specialist experience and the highest standards to meeting clients' needs.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide the highest quality personal service and value to our clients.
